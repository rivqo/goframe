Our Missifon

GoFrame was created to provide Go developers with a clean, elegant framework for building web applications. Inspired by Laravel's simplicity and expressiveness, GoFrame aims to make web development in Go more accessible and enjoyable.

Our Team

GoFrame is maintained by a team of passionate Go developers who believe in creating high-quality, well-documented, and easy-to-use software. We're committed to continuous improvement and responding to the needs of our community.

Why GoFrame?

  • Simplicity: GoFrame provides a clean, intuitive API that makes web development straightforward.
  • Performance: Built on Go's excellent performance characteristics, GoFrame adds minimal overhead.
  • Flexibility: Use as much or as little of the framework as you need.
  • Batteries Included: Authentication, ORM, migrations, and more are included out of the box.

Get Involved

GoFrame is an open-source project, and we welcome contributions from the community. Whether you're fixing bugs, adding features, or improving documentation, your help is appreciated.
